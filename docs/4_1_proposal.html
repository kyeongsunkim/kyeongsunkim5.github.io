23.1 1차년도: 해상풍력발전기 고유진동수의 영향인자 분석

23.1.1 세굴영향 고유진동수 분석을 위한 정밀 수치모델링 기술 개발

23.1.1.1 지반물성값의 비선형성, 지반-지지구조 인터페이스, 풍력타워 구조체 등 시스템의 고유진동수를 정밀하게 모사하는 유한요소기반 수치모델링 기술 개발

To model natural frequency, solid element can be converted to beam elements, Boundary conditions can be applied as linear springs of soil stiffness. Interface properties are not needed.

For 3D modelling, only foundation can be modelled for application of static load. From load-displacement response, stiffness can be obtained, which can be used for calculation of natural frequency.

  • Physical domain effect:
    • 2D axis-symmetric vs 3D
  • Boundary condition effect
    • size of soil box
  • Mesh quality analysis
    • aspect ratio, skewness, sensitivity, computational cost
  • Force method vs Displacement method
  • Initial soil stress
    • effect of compaction or dilation

23.1.1.2 세굴 깊이에 따른 지지력과 축방향 및 회전방향 지반강성 분석을 통해 고유진동수의 변화 확인

We must select important influence factors for natural frequency: f_n = \sqrt{\frac{K}{M}}.

Vesic (1961) provided a relationship between the modulus of subgrade reaction K, used in the Winkler spring problem and the material properties in the elastic continuum problem as K = \frac{0.65 E_s}{(1-\mu^2_s)} \big( \frac{E_s D^4}{E_p I_p} \big)^{1/12} where E_s= soil modulus of elasticity;\mu_s= Poisson’s ratio of soil; D= diameter; and E_p I_p flexural rigidity of pile.

For circular footing embedded in stratum over bedrock, static stiffness of soil (DNV-OS-J101-2014 Table G-2) is given by: K_V = \frac{4GR}{1-\nu} (1+ 1.28 \frac{R}{H}) (1+ \frac{D}{2R}) (1+ (0.85 -0.28 \frac{D}{R})\frac{\frac{D}{H}}{1-\frac{D}{G}})

K_{soil} depends on the following parameters:

  • embedment depth \frac{D}{B}
  • depth to bedrock H
  • Elastic properties G, \nu
  • density of soil \rho
  • tower’s flexural rigidity EI

Check: - relationship between axial, horizontal, and rotational stiffness

  • dominance and interdependence or correlation

23.1.2 원심모형을 통한 석션기초 고유진동수의 세굴영향 분석

23.1.2.1 가속도, 변형률, 변위 등 다양한 결과를 이용한 고유진동수 측정방법 분석

  • accelerometers: perform modal analysis to get dynamic properties (modal shape, natural frequency, damping coeffieicent, etc)

  • strain gauge: wind frequency, fatigue analysis

  • displacement: mode shape verification, load-displacement curve -> stiffness -> natural frequency

23.1.2.2 지반, 석션기초(Mono, Tripod), 상부타워 모형체 및 하중재하 장치 제작

  • soil bed: soil raining technique (use KAIST automatic raining machine)

  • mono vs tripod

  • loading device: eccentric loading (wind only, wave only, wind+wave)

23.1.2.3 세굴깊이에 따른 지지력 변화, 고유진동수 민감도 분석 및 중요인자 결정

  • \frac{m_{nacelle}}{m_{tower}}
  • \frac{m_{tower}}{m_{soil}}
  • \frac{K_{tower}}{K_{soil}}
  • \frac{L_{tower}}{L_{soil}}

23.1.3 다양한 변수조건을 고려한 원심모형시험 수행

23.1.3.1 석션기초 제원(Mono, Tripod), 지반조건(모래, 점토), 풍력발전기 규모 등의 다양한 영향인자를 고려한 변수연구 수행

  • density of soil
    • silt: loose, medium, dense
    • sand: loose, medium, dense
  • size of wind turbine: 3MW or 10MW (scale effect)
    • scale effect may depend on non-homogeneity of material and rigidity of soil profile.
  • Variable cross section of tower structure
  • Mono vs Tripod: spacing
    • spacing influences changes in properties of equivalent-beam model

23.1.3.2 정밀 수치모델링 및 원심모형실험 결과와 비교하여 적용성 검증 및 고도화

  • comparison with international standards: DNV and API equations
  • comparison with FE and centrifuge results
  • comparison with other researcher’s results
